Stage Guide

Know what stage she is in, and what to do next.

01

Early Labor (2-6 hours)

Restlessness, tail raising, reduced appetite, and frequent lying down/standing up.

  • Move cow to a clean, quiet calving pen.
  • Prepare gloves, disinfectant, and emergency vet contacts.
02

Active Labor (30 min-4 hours)

Water bag appears, abdominal contractions become visible, calf presentation begins.

  • Observe every 20-30 minutes.
  • Call vet if no progress after 2 hours or abnormal calf position appears.
03

Placenta Expulsion (up to 12 hours)

After calf delivery, membranes are expelled and cow begins postpartum recovery.

  • Provide warm clean water and monitor temperature.
  • Contact vet if placenta remains beyond 12 hours.
