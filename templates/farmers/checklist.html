Field Checklist

Best-practice routine for safer calving outcomes.

Pre-Calving Setup

  • Disinfect pen and keep dry bedding ready.
  • Record expected calving date range.
  • Prepare emergency kit and sterile ropes.

Calf Immediate Care

  • Clear airway and stimulate breathing if needed.
  • Dip navel in iodine.
  • Ensure first colostrum feed within 2 hours.

Mother Recovery

  • Check bleeding, appetite, and hydration.
  • Observe udder and first milk flow.
  • Log postpartum notes for vet review.
